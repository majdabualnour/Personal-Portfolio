About Me

At just 18 years old, I'm Majd Adel Abualnour - a self-taught programmer, AI enthusiast, and tech entrepreneur from Gaza. With over 6 years of experience in artificial intelligence and microcontroller programming, I've turned my passion for technology into powerful solutions that make a real difference.

Growing up in a challenging environment, I discovered early on that technology could be my gateway to a world of possibilities. What started as a curious teenager tinkering with code has blossomed into founding Magic Tech MT, where we've developed innovative solutions impacting over 200,000 people in my community. Our work with organizations like World Central Kitchen and the World Food Programme has revolutionized aid distribution systems, increasing efficiency by nine times.

My journey hasn't been without obstacles, but each challenge has only fueled my determination. From winning national competitions and earning a trip to NASA, to publishing my book on Computer Vision and creating the Dashxslash platform that provides free education to students worldwide, I've learned that perseverance and innovation can overcome even the most difficult circumstances.

Today, I'm not just a programmer - I'm a mentor to 150+ aspiring developers, a published author, and a firm believer in the power of technology to create positive change. My work in AI and IoT has earned international recognition, but my true passion lies in using these skills to empower others and build a better future for my community.
